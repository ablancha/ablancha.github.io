I am a Senior Scientist at AIR Worldwide where I am building the next generation of AI models for climate risk.

Prior to joining AIR, I was a Postdoctoral Associate at MIT where I use machine learning to help predict extreme events in physical systems. I was part of the SANDLab led by Themis Sapsis.

Before MIT, I was a Ph.D. student at UIUC where I worked on model reduction for fluid–structure interaction problems. I was co-advised by Lawrence Bergman and Alexander Vakakis, and worked closely with Arne Pearlstein.

Research interests:
Machine learning
Dynamical systems
Extreme events
Computational physics
Uncertainty quantification
